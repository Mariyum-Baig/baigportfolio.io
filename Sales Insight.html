Sales Insights

üè¢ Company Overview:

The Sales Dashboard is designed to help organizations track and analyze their sales performance, customer behavior, and market trends. It provides valuable insights into key sales metrics, such as revenue, sales growth, conversion rates, and customer acquisition, to enable data-driven decision-making for improving sales strategies and achieving business goals

üìÇ About the Dataset:

The dataset spans across a defined period (e.g., 2015 to 2023) and includes the following key components:

  • Total sales, revenue, and units sold across different time periods (monthly, quarterly, yearly).
  • Customer demographics, acquisition sources, customer retention, and churn rates.
  • Metrics related to individual and team sales targets, quotas, and achievements.
  • Sales by product, category, or region, including pricing, promotions, and discounts applied.
  • Performance by different sales channels (e.g., online, in-store, telesales).
